INCEPTION

CLUSTER MANAGEMENT

Intelligent cluster management tailored for continuous optimization with the goal of dramatically reducing direct spending on computing resources.

Run-time changes is occured in compliance with fine-grained client requirements (any measurable metrics).

Deliver your genius application, manage builds and orchestrate deployed services through secured, isolated software defined networks across multiple data centers.

No service providers lock: mix public/private clouds with bare-metal boxes, migrate between without interruptions. It's possible now.

Automated benchmarks of specific workloads and live migration in the best possible performance/cost environment.

Flexible insightful reporting, monitoring and projections keeps you gently informed without overwhelming by numbers.

In fact, only value derived from applications and shipped to your customers does matter. We are take care about all underlying headaches.

UNITED

APPLICATION PLATFORM

By nature, any application - it's all about data. Input data from various sources is validated, stored, transformed, aggregated, and sent back as output.

In this growing and globally interconnected world, applications should be distributed, scalable, and dynamic self-organized for delivery high-level of personal user experience.

Build data-intensive real-time applications from composable parts: business logic and algorithms, data-transformation pipelines and flows, machine learning insights and predictions, integrations with external systems, and much more.

Use and share pieces of applications on the marketplace: curated data-streams, services, and algorithms. Let end-users choose exactly what they need and pay only for what they consumed. Don't be greedy, but greatly brings valuable things.

Everything is tied together and acts as a united intelligent system at any scale.

No limits that you can achieve. Just create, reuse, compose and share the best parts. We'll do all the rest.

TRINITY

SELF-CODING INTELLIGENCE

The root reason for building software is to solve complex problems. In the most cases, this is a business problem, wherein the solutions create value.

The create-delivery-profit loop exists as long as your venture has the time (and ability) to follow the changes dictated by new market challenges and opportunities. Our excessively fast, modern world makes it so that our capabilities are insufficient to keep up.

Application development becomes an even more complicated puzzle than the original problem.

Inception covers the management of computer resources in a cost efficient manner with sustainable overall performance for any kind of workloads.

United Application Platform offers a reinvented approach and a set of tools for building composable distributed data-intensive applications.

Trinity opens the new era of applications by looking beyond the origin scope and turns the world changes into the new, not existing before, pieces of software. A self-coding universe that never becomes obsolete and keeps your dreams at night.
